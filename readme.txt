Run command: make speedy

Default mode is 1->Wire frame
Press 1 for Wireframe Mode
Press 2 for Rasterizer Mode
Press 3 for Raytracer Mode
Press 4 for Antialiasing Mode
Press 5 for Culling Mode
Press 6 for Wu Lines Mode
Press 7 for Jump Animation

For Light Positioning
Press U to move the light up
Press J to move the light left
Press G to move the light right
Press H to move the light down
Press B to move the light forward
Press N to move the light forward

For Camera Positioning
Press the up arrow key to move the camera up
Press <- to move the camera left
Press -> to move the camera right
Press the down arrow key to move the camera down
Press W to move the camera forward
Press S to move the camera backward
Press X for rotation along the x axis
Press Y for rotation along the y axis
Press Z for rotation along the z axis

For other features
Press V for a screenshot
Press P for coordinates of light source
Press R to reset the scene
Press C to clear screen
Press L to lookAt the whielight
